# HR Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes employee attrition patterns to identify the major factors influencing employee turnover.

The dashboard focuses on:
- department-level attrition
- overtime impact
- employee satisfaction
- income-level analysis
- age-group attrition trends
- job-role risk analysis

The project was designed to simulate a real-world HR analytics business case.

---

## Business Problem

Employee attrition creates:
- hiring costs
- productivity loss
- operational instability

The objective of this project was to identify:
- high-risk employee groups
- major attrition drivers
- retention improvement opportunities

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview
Includes:

### 2. Root Cause Analysis
Includes:
- overtime vs department analysis
- overtime vs satisfaction analysis

---

## Key Insights

- Employees working overtime show nearly 3x higher attrition
- Sales and Human Resources departments have the highest attrition rates
- Employees aged 18–25 experience the highest turnover
- Lower-income employees show significantly higher attrition
- Sales Representatives and Laboratory Technicians are the highest-risk job roles
- Attrition increases significantly when overtime and low satisfaction occur together

---

## Recommendations

- Reduce overtime dependency in high-risk departments
- Improve retention strategies for younger employees
- Strengthen employee engagement initiatives
- Review compensation structure for lower-income roles
- Monitor overtime workload regularly
